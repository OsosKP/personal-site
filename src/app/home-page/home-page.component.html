About Me

I soon decided I wanted to accomplish more, and moved to Dublin, Ireland to pursue a degree in computer science at University College Dublin. I will be graduating in the summer of 2020 and moving to the east coast of the United States to work as a software engineer.

In my studies at UCD, I've mainly focused on machine learning and web applications. From a language standpoint the program is primarily focused around Java, with occasional detours into others like Python, Ruby and C. However, I have worked to obtain significant extracurricular experience in other technologies. My main focus has been on learning JavaScript with Node.js, Angular and React Native, though in my final year I will be devoting my time to machine learning with Python and TensorFlow 2.
